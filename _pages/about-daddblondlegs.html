A little background

2010

First time to China.
A life-changing experience
that had an enduring
impact on being abroad.

2012

Studied in Shanghai.
Met someone who had
cycled the world
which stuck with me.

2013

First time expat as
an English teacher in Xi'an,
China. Scuba diving in
Thailand, motorcycling across
Vietnam, all sorts of
weirdness, the year
reads like a storybook.

2014

Moved 1,700 miles
to teach at
Fuqing Normal University.
With so much free time
I travelled all over
and eventually cycled around Taiwan
-my first ever bike tour.

2016

Lived in Korea for
4 months.Cycled across
Japan.

2016

Started Master's degree
in Norway. Absolutely
miserable place.

2017

Studied abroad in
France. Back to good
food, good weather,
and good prices
Best decision of my vie.

2019

The devil you know.
Worked at Huawei in Shenzhen.
Travelling was mostly by train.
Started making stupid little videos

2020

Despite Covid, I
knew that if I
ever wanted to actually
cycle across the world
, I'd have to start
cycling. Rented bikes
in other cities
on national holidays.

2021

Bike trips once a
month on average.
Bought a bike.
Took it on trains
and cycled in rural
China.

2022

10 years after
being inspired to
ride around the
world, I wheel
out of Seattle
Airport.
