It all started...

2010

First time to China. A life-changing experience showing the world is full of hidden places.

2012

Studied in Shanghai. Met someone who cycled the world - which always stuck with me.

2013

Became expat teaching in Xi'an, China. Scuba diving in Thailand, motorcycling across Vietnam, all sorts of weirdness, the year reads like a storybook.

2014

Moved 1,700 miles to teach at Fuqing Normal University. Roaming became a lifestyle with all the freetime. First ever bike tour: 19 days around Taiwan

2016

Lived in Korea for 4 months. Cycled 22 days across Japan.

2016

Started Master's degree in Norway. Stunning, posh, miserable.

2017

Studied abroad (from Norway) in France. Back to good food, good weather, and good prices. Best decision of my vie.

 

2019

The devil you know. Worked at Huawei in Shenzhen. Started making stupid little videos.

2020

Despite Covid, I knew that if I ever wanted to cycle around the world, I'd have to start cycling. Rented bikes for tours over national holidays.

 

2021

Cycling filled my weekends. Bought a bike and took it on trains to rural China.

2022

10 years after being inspired to ride around the world, I wheeled out of Seattle Airport with Winnie Mae.
